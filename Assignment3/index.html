Renee Kiffin: My Academic Journey

My Journey

I started my tertiary education at Broward College where I studied Graphic Design. This wasn't my first decision for what I had hoped to study while in college, but nevertheless it was what was available at the time. While doing my Associates in Graphic Design, I joined my school's literary and arts magazine. After completing my Associates in Graphic Design, I decided to continue my education and get my Bachelor's degree. I took a year off from school and worked as a Graphic Design assistant in a small print shop. During this time I applied to many Florida universities and finalized my decision of attending UCF in 2017 to study Digital Media.

Why did you make the decisions along the way which you did?

I decided to attend UCF because I wanted to be better qualified for the jobs I was interested in. My decision was ultimately made based on affordability.
